Clinical Specialities & Services

Evidence-based diagnosis, joint preservation therapies, and precision surgical solutions.

Joint Pain Management

Chronic joint pain, especially in weight-bearing joints, can significantly affect quality of life. We specialize in diagnosing arthritis (osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, gouty arthritis) and establishing non-surgical preservation programs. Treatments include customized exercises, joint supplements, viscosupplementation, and guidance on lifestyle modifications.

Arthritis Care Knee Lubrication Injection Non-Surgical Preservation Gout Management

Sports Injury Care

Sports activities often expose joints to heavy twisting forces, leading to meniscus tears, ligament tears (ACL, PCL, MCL), shoulder dislocations, and ankle instability. Dr. Rahul Shukla offers thorough physical evaluations, MRI reviews, and recovery courses, helping athletes return to their sports safely and efficiently.

ACL / PCL Assessment Meniscus Tear Management Shoulder Dislocation Care Ligament Rehab Plans

Trauma & Fracture Treatment

Accidents and falls require prompt, precise fracture stabilization to prevent long-term joint deformity. We manage all types of fractures, including simple closed fractures, complex multi-fragment breaks, and osteoporotic fragility fractures. Services range from plaster casts and splints to surgical internal fixation (plates and nails).

Bone Fracture Casts Plating & Nailing Surgery Trauma Reconstruction Joint Dislocation Reduction

Knee & Hip Replacement Consultation

When conservative treatments fail and joint cartilage is completely worn down, joint replacement surgery offers reliable pain relief. We perform comprehensive clinical evaluations to determine if you are a candidate for Total Knee Replacement (TKR) or Total Hip Replacement (THR), explaining the procedures, implant choices, and expected outcomes clearly.

Total Knee Replacement (TKR) Total Hip Replacement (THR) Implant Quality Guidance Pre-Surgical Clearance

Arthroscopy Consultation

Arthroscopy is a minimally invasive technique that uses a tiny camera to visualize and repair joint structures. This keyhole approach minimizes tissue damage and speeds up recovery. We provide consultation and post-operative management for arthroscopic repairs of the knee, shoulder, and ankle.

Minimally Invasive Joint Care Keyhole Ligament Reconstruction Shoulder Cuff Assessment Diagnostic Arthroscopy

Physiotherapy & Post-Surgery Care

A successful orthopedic surgery is only half the battle; proper rehabilitation is crucial to restore full joint movement and muscle strength. We design structured physical therapy regimens tailored to your specific surgery or recovery phase, providing hands-on wound care, dressing changes, suture removal, and progress monitoring.

Post-Operative Rehabilitation Suture & Staple Removal Sterile Wound Dressing Joint Range of Motion Exercises
