Clinical Profile

Dr. Rahul Shukla

Dr. Rahul Shukla holds professional medical degrees of MBBS and MS in Orthopedics. Operating out of Napier Town in Jabalpur, he has earned a stellar reputation for precision surgery and empathetic patient care, holding a consistent 5.0 Google rating based on verified patient testimonies.

His clinic, Shraddha Joint & Sports Clinic, is designed to offer patients a clean, professional hospital-like environment for outpatient consults. We prioritize evidence-based diagnostics, avoiding over-prescription, and focusing heavily on long-term joint rehabilitation.

Clinical Approach

We believe that surgery is not always the first step. Our clinical roadmap focuses on conservative management, physiological therapy, joint lubrication, and muscle strengthening before recommending surgical intervention. When surgery is necessary—such as in complex trauma fractures or severe osteoarthritis—we employ standard, modern, high-precision procedures.

Areas of Core Focus

Joint Replacements

Advanced consultations for total knee replacement (TKR) and hip arthroplasty to manage end-stage arthritis.

Sports Injuries

Keyhole/arthroscopy consulting for ligament restoration (ACL/PCL) and torn cartilage repair.

Trauma Reconstruction

Surgical plating, nailing, and casting management for multi-fragment bone fractures and joint dislocations.

Geriatric Bone Care

Diagnosis and support for age-related osteoporosis, chronic back stiffness, and joint lubrication therapies.
